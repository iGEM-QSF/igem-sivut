Project


Imagine if you could feel the scent of a forest, the sea, or city streets in the theatre or cinema.

What if you could adjust the atmoshpere of scents at your home with a remote controller?

Would you be surprised if your hotel room smelled like a grandma's cottage or a castle?

We do research to make these applications feasible!
