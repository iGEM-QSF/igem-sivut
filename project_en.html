PROJECT

"Synthetic biology is a field in biotechnology in which cells and parts of the genome are viewed as building blocks. Using these parts, researchers are trying to build new living systems." -HS 25.4.2014

iGEM or International Genetically Engineered Machine) is an international synthetic biology competition, which has it's 10-year anniversary this year. We are the first ever Finnish iGEM-team.

Our goal is to develop bacteria which produce a particular scent and create a system for controlling it. Our device can be used in scent marketing and creating a unique atmosphere. We will put the bacteria in a container appropriate for the scent. For example, the scent of the forest could be produced in a container shaped like a nest box.

Our objectives are:

  1. Student-driven applied research
  2. Improving the knowledge in synthetic biology
  3. Promoting interdisclipinary and cross-university student cooperation
  4. Opening international networking, cooperation and competition opportunities
  5. Promoting synthetic biology as a science
  6. Improving research and business opportunities among students
